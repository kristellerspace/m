Trip to Boston

Posted on March 10, 2005

ride was looonnggggg omg so boring, but its wtvr cuz we relaxed for that night,i did a lil shakeout run & then went 2 get sum to eat for dinner & stayed at the hotel for the rest of the night. we wanted 2 do smth together, but we couldnt plan it out :(. after waking up the next morning we headed over to the track, TONS of time before our race. i saw sum insane performances!!! SO MANY RECORDS BROKEN & all the things you would expect at a meet like this!!!! our event came ‘round, & we ran 8:11, kinda avg if im being honest. but still boston was super cool and id love to go back!!!
